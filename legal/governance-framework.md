# Governance Framework

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Overview

MESSY operates through dual-layer governance:

- **DAO (Token Holders):** Strategic decisions via on-chain voting (binding)  
- **Association (Messy Virgo DAO):** Swiss legal entity executing DAO decisions

**Key Documents:**

- [Articles of Association](https://github.com/messyvirgo-coin/messyvirgo-org/blob/main/legal/articles-of-association.md)
- [Founding Protocol](https://github.com/messyvirgo-coin/messyvirgo-org/blob/main/legal/2026-01-04-founding-protocol.md)
- [Repository Governance](https://github.com/messyvirgo-coin/messyvirgo-org/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md)

## Legal Entity

**Name:** Messy Virgo DAO  
**Form:** Swiss non-profit association (Art. 60-79 ZGB)  
**Seat:** Zürich, Switzerland  
**Founded:** January 4, 2026  
**Purpose:** Execute community decisions, manage treasury, represent MESSY legally

## Governance Bodies

### 1\. DAO (Token Holders)

**Role:** Strategic direction and major decisions  
**Power:** Binding votes on budget, partnerships, protocol changes  
**Mechanism:** On-chain voting (Snapshot, governance contracts)

### 2\. Board of Directors (2 members)

**Role:** Execute DAO decisions, daily operations  
**Structure:** President (Switzerland) \+ Board Member (International)  
**Signature Authority:**

- Individual: \<CHF 25,000  
- Joint (both required): \>CHF 25,000

### 3\. General Assembly

**Role:** Board elections, constitutional changes  
**Composition:** Association members (one vote each)  
**Meeting:** Annual \+ extraordinary as needed

## Decision Tiers

| Tier | Amount | Timeline | Approval | Signature |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **1: Board** | \<CHF 25k | Immediate | Within budget | Individual |
| **2: Notice** | CHF 25k-50k | 48 hours | Community may object | Joint |
| **3: Vote** | CHF 50k-200k | 8 days | DAO vote (5% quorum, \>50%) | Joint |
| **4: Major** | \>CHF 200k | 14 days | DAO vote (10% quorum, \>50%) | Joint |
| **5: Constitutional** | Any | 21 days | DAO \+ General Assembly (\>66%) | Joint \+ GA |

**Tier Details:**

**Tier 1:** Routine operations, security patches, compliance  
**Tier 2:** Medium spending, non-strategic partnerships; 5% token holders can escalate  
**Tier 3:** Large spending, strategic partnerships, protocol changes (3-day discussion \+ 5-day vote)  
**Tier 4:** Very large spending, annual budget, major upgrades (7-day discussion \+ 7-day vote)  
**Tier 5:** Governance structure changes, Articles amendments (14-day discussion \+ 7-day vote)

## Annual Budget Process

**Q4 Each Year:**

1. Board proposes annual budget (CHF by category)  
2. 30-day community discussion  
3. DAO votes (Tier 4\)  
4. Board operates within approved limits

**Quarterly:** Board reports spending vs. budget

## Accountability

**Board Must:**

- Execute DAO votes within 30 days  
- Report all decisions quarterly  
- Justify non-execution (only if illegal or technically impossible)

**Community Can:**

- Challenge Tier 1-2 decisions retroactively  
- Escalate decisions to higher tiers  
- Remove Board via General Assembly (\>66% vote)

## Emergency Actions

**Board may act immediately for:**

- Critical security exploits  
- Urgent legal compliance (hard deadlines)  
- Active treasury threats

**Process:**

1. Act immediately  
2. Public notification within 24 hours (with evidence)  
3. Retroactive 3-day ratification vote

**If Ratification Fails:**

- Board explains within 7 days  
- 3 failed ratifications/year → General Assembly review  
- Only General Assembly can remove Board (\>66% vote, due process)

**Limits:** Maximum 4 emergency actions/year without GA approval

## Membership

**Token Holders ≠ Association Members** (separate)

**Association Membership:**

- Apply: [legal@messyvirgo.com](mailto:legal@messyvirgo.com)
- Rights: Vote in General Assembly, elect Board
- Encouraged: Major token holders, active contributors

## Proposal Process

**For DAO Votes (Tier 3-4):**

1. Post proposal to governance forum  
2. Discussion period (3-7 days depending on tier)  
3. On-chain vote (5-7 days)  
4. Board executes if passed

**For Constitutional Changes (Tier 5):**

1. Formal proposal with legal review  
2. 14-day discussion  
3. DAO vote (advisory) \+ General Assembly vote (binding)
