# Brand assets & trademarks

This repository may include Messy Virgo brand assets (e.g. logos, avatars/mascots, images, and templates).

## Summary (plain English)

- **Brand assets are controlled**: `brand/` exists for transparency and community coordination. Brand assets are not “open source”.
- **Community use (non-commercial) is allowed**: you may use approved Messy Virgo assets for non-commercial community purposes, as long as you do not imply endorsement.
- **Commercial use requires permission**: any commercial use needs written permission.
- **No endorsement / no “on behalf”**: you may not imply you represent Messy Virgo or that Messy Virgo endorses you.
- **Trademarks**: names/logos may be protected by trademark law even if files are publicly accessible.

See [LICENSE](./LICENSE), [NOTICE](./NOTICE.md), and [brand/USAGE.md](./brand/USAGE.md).

## Allowed (typically OK)

- Sharing official links to the repos and website
- Referencing Messy Virgo by name to describe origin (nominative use), without implying affiliation
- Using unmodified logos/graphics from `brand/` in **non-commercial** community contexts (e.g., fan pages, community announcements, educational posts) with attribution

## Not allowed without permission

- Using assets **commercially** (ads, paid products/services, resale, paid token promotions, etc.)
- Modifying brand assets (derivatives, recolors, edits) for redistribution in a way that could confuse people about what is official
- Using Messy Virgo name/logo/avatar in a way that **confuses people** about affiliation or endorsement
- Presenting your fork, document set, or website as official, or acting “on behalf of” Messy Virgo
- Registering confusingly similar names/domains/social handles
