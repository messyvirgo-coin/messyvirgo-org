# Brand asset usage (quick policy)

This folder contains Messy Virgo brand assets (logos, icons, imagery) used for transparency and coordination.

## What you may do (community / non-commercial)

- Use **unmodified** assets in non-commercial community contexts (community posts, event announcements, educational content).
- Link to official Messy Virgo resources and repositories.
- Attribute Messy Virgo and do not imply endorsement.

## What requires written permission (commercial)

Any commercial use requires permission, including:

- Ads or paid promotions
- Paid products/services, subscriptions, or resale
- Using brand assets in a commercial token listing/marketing context

Request permission at `contact@messyvirgo.com`.

## What you may not do

- Imply you represent Messy Virgo, are endorsed by Messy Virgo, or are acting “on behalf of” Messy Virgo.
- Use brand assets in a way that could confuse people about what is official.
- Register confusingly similar names/domains/handles.
