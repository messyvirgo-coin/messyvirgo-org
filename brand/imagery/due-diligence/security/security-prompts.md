# Prompt pack: Messy as token safety inspector (5 risk levels)

Anime illustration. Character: **Messy Virgo**, identical face/hair/proportions to reference, clearly visible (knees-up to chest-up).

Setting: modern slightly futuristic **inspection bay / lab** at night/sunset. Mood: dark poppy‑punk with subtle neon **blue/pink/violet** + soft **warm amber** highlights; glossy glass/metal surfaces, realistic reflections.
p
Messy is a **crypto token safety inspector**. On the table is the “token” symbolized  by ONE hysical artifact: **sealed capsule** / **engine-like core** / **vault module**. No readable text, **no crypto symbols**.

Crypto/finance hints must be **diegetic only** on real screens/reflections/projection from a visible device. No floating HUD, no badges, no watermarks, no currency icons, no random logos. 

Candlestick-Charts must be **financially 100% correct** (green up, red down). Keep charts subtle.

Outfit: sleek inspector-meets-finance (sharp blazer, gloves, minimal tools). Props: pick 1–2 (clipboard, flashlight, magnifier, UV lamp, evidence bag, tamper tape).

---

## Generate 5 images (one per risk level)

Run the prompt 5 times. Each time, keep character/setting consistent and apply exactly one risk level below.

### Risk level A — Very safe (no risks, pristine)

Mood: calm, certified, “nothing to find.”
- Spotless bay; artifact factory-perfect; green device status lights.
- Messy calmly checks off the clipboard.

### Risk level B — Safe (minor quirks, low risk)

Mood: mostly clean, a couple tiny flags.
- Tidy bay with 1–2 tiny imperfections; artifact has a small scuff / slightly wrinkled seal.
- Subtle yellow caution light; Messy points it out.

### Risk level C — Medium risk (suspicious, needs deeper investigation)

Mood: investigative, ambiguous, “something doesn’t add up.”
- Evidence board (abstract/blurred); artifact shows subtle tamper hints under UV.
- Orange task lights; Messy investigates with UV lamp/magnifier.

### Risk level D — Very risky (active red flags, imminent danger)

Mood: containment, urgency, “do not trust this.”
- Caution tape + containment tray; artifact clearly compromised (broken seal / cracked casing / residue).
- Red emergency lights; Messy seals it into evidence.

### Risk level E — Nightmare (catastrophic, extremely unsafe)

Mood: full disaster, “everything is wrong.”
- Smoke/sparks, shattered glass, toppled gear; artifact catastrophically failed and corrupt-glowing.
- Heavy red lighting + slight sickly green spill; Messy documents with shocked determination.
