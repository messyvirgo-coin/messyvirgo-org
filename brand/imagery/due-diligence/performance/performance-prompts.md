# Prompt pack: Messy as token performance analyst (5 market states)

Anime illustration. Character: **Messy Virgo**, identical face/hair/proportions to reference, clearly visible (knees-up to chest-up).

Setting: modern slightly futuristic **trading command center / market observatory** at night. Mood: dark poppy‑punk with subtle neon **blue/pink/violet** + soft **warm amber** highlights; glossy glass/metal surfaces, realistic reflections.

Messy is a **crypto token performance analyst**. On the central pedestal is the "token" symbolized by ONE physical artifact: a **translucent market-pulse crystal** that glows and shifts color to reflect the token's financial state. No readable text, **no crypto symbols**.

Surrounding screens show **candlestick charts** — these are the hero visual element for this set. Crypto/finance hints must be **diegetic only** on real screens/reflections/projections from visible devices. No floating HUD, no badges, no watermarks, no currency icons, no random logos.

Candlestick charts must be **financially 100% correct** (green candles = price went up, red candles = price went down). Charts are prominent and clearly readable in this set.

Outfit: sleek analyst-meets-trader (tailored blazer, data-gloves, minimal tech accessories). Props: pick 1–2 (holographic tablet, price scanner wand, analytics monocle, live-feed earpiece, projection remote).

---

## Generate 5 images (one per market state)

Run the prompt 5 times. Each time, keep character/setting consistent and apply exactly one market state below.

### Market state A — Extreme bull run (parabolic, euphoric)

Mood: triumphant, electric, "this token is on fire."
- Crystal blazing brilliant green‑gold, pulsing with energy; screens filled with steep green candle runs.
- Warm golden light spills across the room; Messy leans back with a wide, beaming smile, tablet showing all-time-high alerts.

### Market state B — Bullish (healthy uptrend, steady growth)

Mood: optimistic, composed, "looking strong."
- Crystal glowing steady soft green; screens show a clean uptrend with mostly green candles and gentle pullbacks.
- Calm teal/green ambient light; Messy studies the charts with a pleased smile, pointing at the upward trajectory.

### Market state C — Sideways (consolidation, mixed signals)

Mood: uncertain, watchful, "could go either way."
- Crystal flickering between pale green and amber; screens show a flat, choppy chart with alternating red and green candles.
- Neutral amber/blue lighting; Messy tilts her head with a curious half-smile, hand on chin, comparing multiple screens.

### Market state D — Bearish (sustained downtrend, bleeding)

Mood: tense, cautious, "this is not looking good."
- Crystal dimming to deep red-orange; screens show a declining staircase of red candles with failed bounces.
- Cold red/blue lighting; Messy keeps a wry, knowing smile while pulling up deeper analytics, body language guarded.

### Market state E — Catastrophic crash (freefall, capitulation)

Mood: full panic, devastation, "everything is collapsing."
- Crystal cracked and flickering violently between sickly red and dark; screens show near-vertical red candle drops, some screens glitching.
- Heavy red lighting with harsh flicker; Messy grips the console edge wide-eyed but still wears a small determined smile — she's seen worse.
